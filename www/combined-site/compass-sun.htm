Finding Compass Directions using Sun and Time

If you know the time, you can use the Sun as a Compass.

  • Simply face your shadow, and then North will be the angle of your hour-hand on your watch.

At the Equinoxes (Winter Equinox is March 20th, Summer Equinox is September 23rd), the sun rises exactly due East and sets exactly due West.
